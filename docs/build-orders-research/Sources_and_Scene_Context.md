## Who's who — the current top players cited

| Player | Race | Why they matter (2025–2026) |
|---|---|---|
| **Soma (Park Sang-hyun)** | Zerg | **Back-to-back ASL champion** (S20 over Snow 4–2; S21 over Flash 4–3). The single best "recent meta" anchor — his ZvP 5-Hatch-Hydra family and aggressive ZvT/ZvZ define current Zerg. |
| **Flash (이영호, Lee Young-ho)** | Terran | The benchmark Terran ("GOAT"). ASL21 finalist (May 2026). His Double-Armory TvP and 5-Factory Goliath TvZ are *the* Terran macro standards. |
| **Bisu (Kim Taek-yong)** | Protoss | Defined modern PvZ (the Corsair/DT "Bisu Build" and its successors). ASL20 semifinalist. Prefers Arbiters in PvT lategame. |
| **Best, Snow** | Protoss | Top current Protosses. Best = ASL19 finalist; Snow = ASL20 finalist. Both open FFE in PvZ and 1-Gate-Core in PvT/PvP. |
| **Soulkey, Jaedong** | Zerg | Soulkey won ASL19. Jaedong (the legendary "Tyrant") still competing in ASL21. |
| **Light, Rush, Bbyong** | Terran | Active top Terrans across ASL19–21. "Light's Fast Arbiter" guide is actually a Protoss build attributed to a different context — see note in main doc. |

## How to keep this current yourself

1. **Watch the newest ASL season** on the official YouTube/SOOP channels — the openings you see are the live meta.
2. **TL.net `bw-strategy` forum**, sorted by recent — for written discussion and replays.
3. **Liquipedia** for the canonical step-by-step orders (stable; openings rarely change).
4. **Korean streamers on SOOP/AfreecaTV** (Flash, Bisu, etc.) stream builds and teaching almost daily — search YouTube for "{종족}전 빌드 강의".
